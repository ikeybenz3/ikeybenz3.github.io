OUR MISSION

Changing lives, one wave at a time.

Sheila Matut and Geri Cohen are the cofounders of JSC.

The have been surfing for 8 years and have been inspired by what the surf culture has to offer.

A big portion of their inspiration would be their older brothers who have influenced them to be immersed in their upbringing in Deal, New Jersey.

They spend a lot of their time year round in the water, and want to share the passion surfing gave them with their campers.

Geri and Sheila are both Red Cross certified lifeguards and ensure their campers will be completely taken care of in and out of the water.

A Great Summer Awaits.
